resource level deletion cascade policy

[x] franchise : all users of franchise, timings, review, commission policy, all customer of franchise, all vehicles of those customers, all bookings of those customers, booking items, invoice and payments related to that booking
[x] user : all bookings managed by that user, all booking items, invoices and payments related to those bookings
[x] customer : all of its vehicles, all bookings of the customer, booking items, invoices, payments related to those bookings, all franchise reviews of that customer
[x] vehicle : all bookings related to it, booking items, invoices, payments related to those bookings
[x] booking : all booking items, invoice and payments of that invoice
[x] invoice (exception) : the booking related to it, all booking items of that booking, and all payments related the invoice
[x] booking item & payment : can not be deleted standalone
[x] service : can not be deleted : only be deactivated
